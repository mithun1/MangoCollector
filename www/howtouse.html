How to Play

In this game you have to collect mango as per instruction. In your First level you have to collect six mango for complete the level and go for the next level. Your total speed is limited so you have to spent speed as per your need to collect the mango. Every time you through a stone towords the mango same amount of speed deduced from your total speed(given to you at the start of the level). You also increase or decrease angle as per you need there is no restriction.Never go for a negative angle.

When the color of the total speed is red never press the play button. First decrease the speed and when the red become green then you could press the play button(the button of the center for throw stone towards mango).

First level total speed 3000 you have to collect six mango for go to the next level.

In you second level total speed 2700 you have to collect six mango for go to the next level.

In you second level total speed 2500 you have to collect six mango for Complete the game.
